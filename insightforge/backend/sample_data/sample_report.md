# AI in Healthcare Industry Intelligence Report (Global)

## Executive Summary
- The global AI in healthcare market shows strong expansion driven by clinical automation and workflow optimization [1].
- Consensus estimates indicate double-digit CAGR through 2030, with payer-provider adoption accelerating [2].

## Market Overview
Healthcare systems are scaling AI use cases across diagnostics, revenue cycle, and patient engagement.

## Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- TAM: USD 180.0B [1]
- SAM: USD 92.5B [2]
- SOM: USD 22.4B [3]

## CAGR Forecast
Projected CAGR: 14.2% for 2024-2030 [4].

## Market Drivers
- Provider labor shortages and burnout mitigation [5]
- Need for outcome-based care models [6]

## Market Restraints
- Data privacy and model governance complexity [7]
- Integration friction with legacy systems [8]

## Trends
- Clinical foundation models
- Ambient documentation and coding copilots

## Regulatory Landscape (Global)
- AI transparency and risk-classification frameworks are tightening [9]

## Competitive Landscape
Competition is intensifying among platform vendors, cloud hyperscalers, and specialized health AI firms.
